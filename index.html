About

Dr. Kundakarla Bhanu Prasad is a renowned infectious disease specialist based in Hyderabad, India. Currently serving as an Assistant Professor in the Department of General Medicine at Nizam’s Institute of Medical Sciences (NIMS), he completed his DM in Infectious Diseases from Christian Medical College (CMC), Vellore – one of India’s premier institutions for advanced medical training. His research and clinical work focus on the management of complex infections in transplant recipients, the optimization of antimicrobial stewardship programs, and the diagnosis and treatment of invasive fungal infections such as mucormycosis.

Driven by a passion for evidence-based medicine, Dr. Bhanu Prasad actively engages with the latest scientific literature and has published and presented at both national and international conferences. He is committed to improving patient outcomes through early diagnosis, targeted therapy, and multidisciplinary collaboration.

Clinical Expertise

Transplant Infectious Diseases

Management of infections in solid organ and hematopoietic stem cell transplant recipients, including prophylaxis strategies, diagnostic dilemmas, and tailored therapeutics.

Antimicrobial Stewardship

Development and implementation of stewardship programs to optimize antibiotic use, combat antimicrobial resistance, streamline diagnostics, and improve patient safety.

Invasive Fungal Infections

Expertise in the diagnosis and management of invasive fungal diseases, including mucormycosis, candidiasis, aspergillosis, and emerging opportunistic moulds in immunocompromised hosts.
